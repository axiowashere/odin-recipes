Vanilla Ice Cream

Description

This homemade ice cream is easy to make with just 4 ingredients: pure vanilla extract, milk, heavy whipping cream, and sugar. This American or Philadelphia-style ice cream makes for a brighter, more pronounced vanilla flavor.

Original recipe and image from Allrecipes.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Stir milk, cream and sugar in a saucepan on low heat until sugar has dissolved. Heat until a small ring of foam appears around the edge.
  3. Transfer crea mixture to a pourable container. Stir in vanilla extract and chill for at least 2 hours.
  4. Pour cold ice cream ix into an ice cream maker, and churn for 20 to 25 minutes.
  5. Serve directly or place in freezer to ripen for 2 to 3 hours.
